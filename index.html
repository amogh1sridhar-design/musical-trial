Connect Spotify Premium

Play full songs (not 30-second previews) right inside the visualizer. Requires a Spotify Premium account.

  1. Go to developer.spotify.com/dashboard and click Create app (free).
  2. Give it any name. For Redirect URI, paste exactly:
  3. Under "Which API/SDKs", tick Web Playback SDK, save.
  4. Open the app's Settings and copy the Client ID, paste it below.

Your Client ID is stored only in this browser. Visuals on Spotify tracks are driven by Spotify's beat & loudness analysis (their stream is DRM-protected, so live frequency analysis isn't possible).
